About me

Working since 2001 as data analyst and with business intelligence tools, I contributed to initiatives involving systems on these topics, occupying team coordination functions and various technical roles such as modeling, ETL, reporting, analytics frontends, monitoring, application performance and updates, both in new deployments and in maintenance of existing systems.

I participated in accomplishments in Business Intelligence and Data Analytics, adding value to teams and associated projects by a creative and innovative aspect, aware to innovations and presenting new knowledge to the team. Always considering the general picture of the business needs during the creation of solutions to meet data demands for business results evaluation and decision making.

To perform these activities competently, I had to improve an analytical, objective, and methodical mentality to carry out tasks in an organized manner, approach situations logically and seek root causes of challenges and problems found, as well as the ability to instruct people from the technical team and the business area in the use of the resources made available to them.
